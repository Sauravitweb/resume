Ratan Tata: A Visionary Leader's Journey

Ratan Tata, born on December 28, 1937, is one of India's most respected business leaders and philanthropists. He served as the chairman of Tata Sons and expanded the company's reach globally, making Tata Group a name recognized worldwide. Under his leadership, Tata Group acquired several major international companies, including Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel, establishing India's presence in the global business arena. Known for his humility, foresight, and ethical business practices, Ratan Tata has always prioritized the welfare of employees and the community. His philanthropic work, through the Tata Trusts, has touched millions of lives across India, focusing on areas like healthcare, education, and rural development.
